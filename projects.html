My Projects

Check out some of my design projects, meticulously crafted with love and dedication, each one reflecting the passion and soul I poured into every detail.

MONDO IMPORTS – Wine Landing Page

This landing page was created in Figma for a premium wine import brand. The design aims to evoke luxury and sophistication, using deep tones, high-contrast typography, and rich imagery of grapes and wine glasses. The hero section captures the brand’s story while inviting users to explore products or get started. Every element was carefully aligned to convey trust, tradition, and taste.

Plantify – Indoor Plant eCommerce Website

Designed in Figma, Plantify is a clean and modern homepage concept for an indoor plant eCommerce brand. The layout embraces a calming color palette, spacious typography, and a hero section that introduces the brand’s purpose. I focused on creating a warm, nature-inspired aesthetic through minimalistic navigation, balanced white space, and a central product visual to attract eco-conscious users.

QuickBite – Food Delivery App Task Flow

Designed in Figma, QuickBite is a streamlined task flow concept for a modern food delivery app. The design maps the user journey from ordering a food to final order placement. I focused on creating a smooth, intuitive experience with consistent UI patterns, clear visual hierarchy, and purposeful use of icons and colors.

Knowledge Gate University – University Website Design

Designed in Figma, this website concept for Knowledge Gate University focuses on clear structure and intuitive navigation. Starting with wireframes, I built a clean academic layout that showcases courses, internships, and campus life. The design reflects a modern, professional tone and considers multiple user types like applicants, students, and internship seekers.
